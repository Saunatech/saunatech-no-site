1) Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Saunatech uses cookies and similar technologies in connection with our website, applications, and related services (the "Services").

2) What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit or use online services. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, SDK-based storage, and other technologies that store or access information on your device or browser.

We use both essential and optional cookies or similar technologies. At launch, our optional categories are limited to first-party analytics operated by Saunatech and maps-related services that may rely on Google Maps or Google Places.

3) Categories we use

Essential

Required: Yes

Authentication, session management, security, fraud prevention, and core functionality.

First-party analytics

Required: No

Saunatech-operated product analytics, consent-aware event measurement, and service improvement.

Maps

Required: No

Map previews, map interactions, location-related features, and address or place lookups where available via Google Maps / Google Places.

Essential

Essential cookies and similar technologies help us:

  • keep you signed in where applicable,
  • maintain secure sessions,
  • support core booking and account flows,
  • remember core preferences such as language or locale where appropriate, and
  • protect the Services against abuse, fraud, and technical issues.

These technologies are used because they are necessary to operate the Services or because their use is otherwise permitted under applicable law without opt-in consent.

Analytics

Analytics cookies and similar technologies may be used for Saunatech's first-party product analytics. This helps us understand how key features are used, measure funnel transitions, diagnose product issues, and improve the Services. These analytics are recorded through Saunatech-controlled systems rather than a third-party analytics platform.

Where required by law, we use these technologies only after you have given consent.

Maps

Maps-related cookies and similar technologies may be used when we provide map previews, map interactions, location-related features, or address and place lookup functionality. At launch, these features may rely on Google Maps or Google Places.

Where required by law, we use these technologies only after you have given consent.

4) Local storage and session storage

In addition to cookies, we may use local storage or session storage for core flows, such as preserving booking progress, session state, verification state, or a consented first-party analytics session identifier. These technologies are not the same as cookies, but they can serve similar functions.

5) Consent and legal controls

Where required by law, including in the EU/EEA/UK, we ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies. This means:

  • we do not set or use non-essential cookies or similar technologies until you make a choice where consent is required,
  • rejecting non-essential cookies is as easy as accepting them, and
  • you can change your choices later through Cookie Settings.

If you do not consent to optional categories, some optional features may be unavailable or may not function as intended.

6) Managing your preferences

You can manage your preferences at any time through Cookie Settings, such as through the cookie banner, preferences controls, or other available settings in the Services.

Browser settings may also allow you to block or delete cookies. However, blocking essential cookies or similar technologies may affect the operation of the Services.

7) Maps services and future changes

At launch, the only optional third-party cookie-based features we currently rely on are maps and address/place features provided through Google Maps or Google Places where those features are available.

If we introduce additional third-party measurement, embedded services, advertising, or other optional cookie-based features in the future, we will update this Cookie Policy and, where required by law, provide appropriate notice, controls, and consent choices.

8) More information

For more information about how we collect, use, share, and protect personal data, see our Privacy Policy.
